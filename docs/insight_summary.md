# Insight Summary

## Key Findings

The business generated total revenue of 10,349,505.85 across the analysis period.

Lead-to-customer conversion was 28.93%, with 868 customers created from 3,000 leads.

North was the strongest region overall, generating 4,335,457.65 in revenue and outperforming revenue targets in 17 of 23 months. South was the second strongest region, generating 3,419,190.60 in revenue and beating target in 15 of 23 months.

East underperformed target in most months and exceeded target only 3 times, although it showed a strong spike in December 2025. West was the least consistent region, exceeding target only once across the period, despite having the highest average order value in earlier KPI analysis.

A major outlier occurred in December 2025, when all four regions exceeded target, especially North and South. This suggests possible seasonality, year-end deal concentration, or target-setting issues.

## Business Interpretation

Revenue performance was heavily dependent on North and South. East and West showed weaker target attainment, meaning growth was not evenly distributed across regions.

The December 2025 spike materially influenced the overall performance trend and should be treated as a notable exception when interpreting the business.
